Welcome to CopyPasta

CopyPasta is a project that allows you to scan a text (QR Code, Barcode, Text recognition, ...) with your phone and send it to your computer.

How to use

To use this application, you must follow this simple instructions :

  • Download the android application and the Windows software here.
  • Open the Windows sofware and the Android application
  • On your mobile phone, add device in clicing on the button and in scanning the QR Code showed at the screen of your computer.
  • You have finish ! You have just to select the device on your application and you can now use CopyPasta !
