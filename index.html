Welcome to CopyPasta

CopyPasta is a project that allows you to scan a text or an image (QR Code, Barcode, Text recognition, ...) with your phone and send it to your computer.

How to use

To use this application, you must follow this simple instructions :

  • Download the android application and the Windows software here.
  • Open the Windows sofware and the Android application
  • On your mobile phone, add your computer by clicking on the "add a device" button, then scan the QR Code shown on the screen of your computer.
  • It's finished ! You just have to select the newly registered computer on your application and can now use CopyPasta !
